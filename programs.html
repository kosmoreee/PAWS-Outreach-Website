PAWS Cleaning Outreach Program

The goal of this outreach project is to support the advocacy of PAWS through voluntary help by cleaning, donations, and the sharing of knowledge regarding animal welfare in the community.

With only 3 caretakers looking after 300+ shelter animals, keeping the shelter clean can be a challenge. The PAWS Shelter Cleaning Program is a practical, low-budget way to help out at the shelter.

This is a a practical, low-budget way for your group to help out at the shelter.

Shelter Cleaning Program

Marist School

The class of 9-St. Barnabas has reached out to PAWS to participate in the Shelter Cleaning Program, hoping to contribute to the well-being of the rescued animals by providing a clean and comfortable environment for them.

Partnership

The class sees this as an opportunity not only to give back to the community but also to raise awareness about animal welfare and responsible pet ownership. Through their participation, they aim to support the shelter staff in maintaining a safe and hygienic space for the rescued animals, ensuring they receive the care they need.
